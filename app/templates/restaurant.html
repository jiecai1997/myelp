The "MYelp" Difference

Our algorithm does not just provide overall star ratings like Yelp does - it uses NLP to generate specific ratings for categories that are important to users - Food, Ambiance, Price, and Service. MYelp does this by generating category ratings for each individual review based on a sentiment analysis algorithm, and then averaging these values.
